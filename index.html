Welcome to our site!

Hello! We are RDDT Websites, and this is our site! This site is programmed in html and css. It runs on an express.js backend. It is a remodel of our old site, which was designed by GamingLizard9 using Bulma. This one is designed by us, and uses tailwind!





How is this hosted?

We are hosting this on Netlify. We previously used Heroku, but they took down their free teir do to abuse. Netlify is also a lot simpler, and allows us to build and add our express apps. If you try to make a website, we recommend it!

Lawyer Simulator

Lawyer simulator is game where you can a defense attorney or a prosecuting attorney. You must try to convice the Jury why your side should be voted for. If you are a defense attorney, you know everything that happened, and must get the defendent to be not guilty. If you are a prosecuting attorney, you know everything that the cops found out, and must prove why the defendent is guilty.

Cloud Weather

Cloud Weather is brand new weather app. It will feature modern design, high reliablity, and amazing features. It uses OpenWeatherMap and is going to run off an express.js backend. It was abandoned a few months ago once education started up, but is being resumed by Tyler and EngineerFox. They are redesigning it to feature a more eye friendly and simplistic design.
